Math Stack Exchange

I was semi-active on MSE. One of my professors in undergrad, Joel Hamkins, offered extra credit to those who gained at least 100 reputation by the end of the semester. I enjoy looking back at questions I asked involving my calculus homework. I recommend students try answering questions or asking questions of their own for some period of time!


Some of my answers:
  • Trying to solve a recurrence relation by using generating functions: \(a_n = 3a_{n-1} + a_{n-2}\)
  • Proof of König's line coloring theorem ( \( \chi'(G) = \Delta (G) \) )
  • Length of a cycle is \(3k\) if every cycle has length of \(\ge 5\)
