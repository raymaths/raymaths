I received my PhD in 2024 at Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond, Virginia under the direction of Craig E. Larson and Allison H. Moore.

My dissertation: Problems in Graph Theory With Applications to Topology and Modeling RNA
Those who have guided me through undergrad include Kevin O'Bryant, Andrew C. Poje , and Erika L.C. King. Additionally Mikael Vejdemo-Johansson, who is enthusiastic about getting undergrads involved and lending an ear when needed, and Cheryl Craddock and Cynthia Palumbo of the Verrazzano School at CUNY-CSI.
My research interests are primarily graph theory, applications of graph theory, and the intersection of combinatorics and topology.
