I received my PhD in 2024 at Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond, Virginia under the direction of Craig E. Larson and Allison H. Moore.

My dissertation: Problems in Graph Theory With Applications to Topology and Modeling RNA
Those who have guided me through undergrad include Kevin O'Bryant, Andrew C. Poje , Mikael Vejdemo-Johansson, and Erika L.C. King. Additionally Cheryl Craddock and Cynthia Palumbo of the Verrazzano School at CUNY-CSI have been invaluable support figures, both academically and in life.
