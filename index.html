My Ph.D. advisors are Craig E. Larson and Allison H. Moore.
Those who have guided me through undergrad include Kevin O'Bryant, Andrew C. Poje , and Erika L.C. King. Additionally Mikael Vejdemo-Johansson, who is enthusiastic about getting undergrads involved and lending an ear when needed.
My research interests are primarily graph theory, applications of graph theory, and the intersection of combinatorics and topology.
I am semi-active on MSE. One of my professors in undergrad, Joel Hamkins, offered extra credit to those who gained at least 100 reputation by the end of the semester. I enjoy looking back at questions I asked involving my calculus homework.
